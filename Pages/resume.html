SAMA MOAYERI
Freelance Designer

Sama Moayeri born in 1999 is a senior at the University of Tehran (BA, Visual communication). She ranked first in the Nationwide University Entrance Exam for Arts in 2017 and started her artistic career. She has been teaching art history and visual creativity for three years now. Passionate about arts, literature, and history, she also works as a translator.
